Manager takeaway

  • Contract is #1 driver — month-to-month customers churn much more than locked-in contracts.
  • Catch rate is strong — recall ~75.67% means most leavers can be flagged early.
  • Expect some false alarms — precision ~49.65% is acceptable if save offers are low-cost.
  • Focus spend on high-risk, short-tenure, month-to-month customers before month 12.
